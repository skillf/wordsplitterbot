Word splitter successfully installed.

Splits your words into characters.

/wordsplitter <sentence>

Examples:

/wordsplitter LETS GET TO THE GYM FAST
=> 
L E T S   G E T   T O   T H E   G Y M   F A S T


/wordsplitter COME, HELP ME!
=> 
C O M E   H E L P   M E !


/wordsplitter NO MORE LEAVES!
=> 
N O   M O R E   L E A V E S !

That is how interesting Word Splitter is.
